Discover the perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience at Lock Pointe Village —Canal Winchesters’ newest build‑to‑rent community. Thoughtfully designed with single‑level living, upscale finishes, and resort‑style amenities, this is more than a home — it’s a lifestyle.

Inspired by the rich canal heritage of Canal Winchester, Lock Pointe Village blends timeless charm with modern living. Named in tribute to the historic locks that once fueled the region’s growth, our community stands at the intersection of past and present—offering spacious homes, peaceful surroundings, and a fresh take on connected living.
